Taye Arimoro

Taye Arimoro is a professional actor based in Lagos, Nigeria, known for his ability to bring characters to life through his dynamic use of voice, body, actions, and reactions. Taye has a robust portfolio of work in TV, film, and stage productions. He has appeared in notable TV shows such as "Castle & Castle" on Ebonylife TV, where he played a pathologist.

Taye Arimoro was lead actor in Niyi Akinmolayan's "Casa De Novia" on Prime Video, "The House of Secrets", Clarence Peters "Inside Life" on Netflix, Inkblot's latest Nigerian football film "When Love Strikes", Anthill's "Criminal" streaming on Prime Video, "Last Year Single" now streaming on Netflix, Russet TV's "There's Something Wrong with the Bamideles", "Son of the Soil" a Nigerian-UK action thriller directed by Chee Keong (a UK-Chinese director). Taye Arimoro has also been in various television shows including Africa Magic's "Venge", James Omokwe's "Ajoche" and "The Decision", an adaptation of Cinderella.
