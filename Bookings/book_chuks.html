Chuks Joseph

Chuks Joseph has become a fan favorite in the Nollywood space and beyond, admired for his charming, witty, smart, and confident personality. To hone his craft as a professional actor, he trained at renowned film institutes, including EbonyLife Creative Academy, Royal Arts Academy , and the African International Film Festival (AFRIFF) acting masterclass. Currently based in Lagos, Chuks is an accomplished actor and model with a strong presence in the entertainment industry.

Standing 5’9” with an athletic build, Chuks has appeared in numerous blockbuster films (both in cinemas and on streaming platforms), TV shows, music videos, commercials, and fashion shows. His notable works include Dark October, The Origin: Madam Koi-Koi, and Afamefuna on Netflix, Criminal on Prime Video, Leaked and Kokomma on YouTube. He also starred in When Love Strikes, Nigeria’s latest football- themed film. On television, Chuks has featured in series such as Masquerades of Aniedo, Schooled, Nwanyi Ike, Riona, Baby Drama, and Flatmates.

As a model, Chuks was the first runner-up in the 2020 Mr. Ideal Nigeria competition, earning the title of Mr. Ideal Nigeria Earth. He has since been featured in music videos, commercials, and fashion shoots, showcasing his versatility and presence in the modeling world.
