Durotimi Okutagidi

Oluwadurotimi Okutagidi is a talented actor celebrated for his versatility in stage plays, feature-length films, and short movies. A graduate of EbonyLife Creative Academy, he has honed his craft through formal training and practical experience. Beyond acting, Oluwadurotimi has also directed personal projects and stage plays, highlighting his multifaceted skills in theater and film.

His notable acting credits include roles in the upcoming film Everything Scatter directed by Jade Osiberu, Son of the Soil (2024), a Nigerian-UK action thriller by UK-Chinese director Chee Keong, and The Bride Price under Sunom Kura George’s direction. He also stars as James in Mirrors: James’ Split by Arnold Mafo and appeared in Naija Saga (2022) directed by Akinyemi Tag. On stage, Oluwadurotimi delivered compelling performances as Olunde in Death and the King’s Horseman (2022) and as the Second Chief in The Gods Are Not to Blame (2022), both directed by Ayo Ajisafe. In addition to acting, Oluwadurotimi has contributed to projects like the short film Ebiren Re (2024) as Godfrey Williams, directed by Yinka Otubusin, and the TV series Cheta’m (2023) as crew/Apoputara, directed by James Omokwe. With a passion for storytelling and an unwavering dedication to his craft, Oluwadurotimi continues to leave a mark on the entertainment industry.
