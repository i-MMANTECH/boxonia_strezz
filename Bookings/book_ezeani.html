Ezeani Chinaza

Ezeani Chinaza, known as Miss Ezeani, is a multitalented creative and versatile actress known for her enthralling one-woman productions. To further enhance her acting skills, she attended the prestigious Ebonylife Creative Academy to help kickstart her career as an actor.

Ezeani has showcased her talent in various notable projects, including James Omokwe's Chetana (Showmax, 2023) Enclosed (2024), Uriri (African Magic Showcase, 2024), A Trio's Tale (2024), Kokumo (2024), an Ebonylife Creative Academy project. With several exciting projects in the pipeline, including Omoni Oboli's Cupcakes for Val. Beyond acting, she's a successful influencer, collaborating with top brands like Bolt, TikTok, Itel, Jumia amongst others. Miss Ezeani’s impressive range of characters has garnered significant attention online and has made her an internet sensation. She is undoubtedly one to look out for in the entertainment industry.
