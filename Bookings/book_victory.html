Victory Eyong

Victory Eyong is a promising young actress steadily making her mark in the film industry. She made her on-screen debut in 2019 with Boxonia’s short film Otana and has since shown unwavering determination to establish herself as a notable talent.

In 2023, Victory starred in the Nollywood blockbuster Bank Alert, sharing the screen with industry icons like Okey Bakassi, Kanayo O. Kanayo, Kate Henshaw, Taiwo Hassan, and Bolanle Ninolowo. Her passion for acting, coupled with a deep understanding of her craft, positions her as a rising star and a strong contender among her peers.
